Welcome to Balm Beats

Balm Beats is a passion project fueled by a never-ending love for matching movement to music.  My name is Hayley and I am so excited to meet you!  I'm a Philadelphia native (go Eagles!), and after various stints working in Paris, New York, Philly & San Francisco, I currently reside in Las Vegas with my fiancé and my black cat, Bartholomew.  I have been many things: a student, an athlete, a DJ, and an attorney, but by far the most meaningful aspect of my life has been teaching indoor cycling for the past 6 years.  In real life, I am somewhat shy and introverted, but good beats and good sweat make me come alive on the podium and I try to infuse that energy into each and every one of my riders.  I'm also a beauty/cosmetics junkie, hence the "Balm" in "Balm Beats".  Look out for some of my favorite products, favorite tunes and daily musings from a crazy cat lady living in the City of Sin.
