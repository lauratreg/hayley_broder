What's up, I'm Hayley.

Thanks for stopping by...I am so excited to meet you!  I'm a Philadelphia native (go Eagles!), and after various stints working in Paris, New York, Philly & San Francisco, I currently reside in Las Vegas with my husband and our two black cats. 

I have been many things: a student, an athlete, a DJ, and an attorney, but by far the most meaningful aspect of my life has been teaching indoor cycling for the past 7 years.   In "real life", I am somewhat shy and introverted, but good beats and good sweat make me come alive on the podium and I try to infuse that energy into each and every one of my classes.  I'm also a skincare & cosmetics junkie, hence the "Balm" in "Balm Beats".  

In here you'll find great music, recipes for lazy cooks, beauty recommendations and daily musings from a crazy cat lady living in the City of Sin.  All views expressed here are my own.
